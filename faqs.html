FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Common questions about ordering, payment and delivery.

How do I place an order?
Call or WhatsApp us on 073 188 0408, or use the contact form. Tell us what you want—a bed, chair, ottoman or something custom—and we'll quote and guide you through the process.
What payment do you require?
We ask for a 50% deposit to confirm your order. The balance is due on delivery.
How long does production take?
Typically 2–3 weeks from order confirmation. We'll confirm timing when you order.
Can I choose my own fabric and colour?
Yes. All our pieces can be customized in the colour and fabric of your choice. We can help you choose if you like.
Can you make something from a design I found?
Yes. Saw it on Pinterest or elsewhere? Send us a photo and we'll work out a custom quote to make it for you.
Where are you based? Can I visit?
We're at 41 Brand Road, President Park, Midrand. Visits are by appointment only—call or WhatsApp to arrange.
Do you deliver?
Yes. We can arrange delivery, or you can collect from our workshop. Contact us to discuss delivery options and costs.
