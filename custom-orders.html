Custom orders

Made to your vision

Saw it on Pinterest? Have a design in mind? Send us a photo or describe what you want—we'll work out a custom quote and make it for you.

How custom orders work

  1. Get in touch — Call or WhatsApp 073 188 0408, or use our contact form. Tell us what you're looking for or send a design you love.
  2. Quote — We'll provide a quote based on size, design and fabric. We can help you choose colours and materials.
  3. Deposit — A 50% deposit secures your order.
  4. Production — Your piece is made in our workshop. Production typically takes 2–3 weeks.
  5. Delivery — Balance is due on delivery. We deliver or you can collect from our workshop in Midrand.
