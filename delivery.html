Delivery & payment

How we handle production, payment and getting your furniture to you.

Payment

  • 50% deposit — Required to confirm your order and start production.
  • Balance on delivery — The remaining 50% is due when your piece is delivered or collected.

Production time

Production typically takes 2–3 weeks from when your order is confirmed. We'll keep you updated. Place your order in good time if you need your piece by a specific date.

Delivery & collection

We can arrange delivery, or you can collect from our workshop at 41 Brand Road, President Park, Midrand. Collection is by arrangement. Contact us to discuss delivery options and costs for your area.
