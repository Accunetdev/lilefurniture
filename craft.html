Our craft

Made to order, made to last

Every piece is custom-made in our workshop. We work with quality materials and take care with every detail.

How it works

  1. 1

    Consultation

    Get in touch by phone or WhatsApp. Tell us what you're looking for—a bed, chair, ottoman or something unique. Send a design you love or describe your vision.

  2. 2

    Quote & deposit

    We'll provide a quote based on size, design and fabric. A 50% deposit secures your order. We'll help you choose colours and fabrics if you like.

  3. 3

    Production

    Your piece is made in our workshop. Production typically takes 2–3 weeks. We build to last, with attention to structure and finish.

  4. 4

    Delivery

    Balance is due on delivery. We'll arrange delivery or you can collect from our workshop in Midrand. See Delivery info for more.

Materials & finishes

Upholstery

We use quality fabrics—linens, velvets and other textiles—in a range of neutral and custom colours. You choose the fabric and colour for your piece.

Structure & frames

Frames are built for stability and longevity. Where visible, we use wood tones and finishes that complement the overall design.

Details

From channel-tufted headboards to ribbed ottomans and clean-lined chairs, we focus on details that make each piece distinctive and comfortable.
