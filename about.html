About us

Bringing your furniture ideas to life

We create beautiful, custom furniture—from beds and headboards to occasional chairs and statement sofas. Every piece is made in our workshop to your specifications.

Our story

Lile Furniture is a custom furniture workshop based in President Park, Midrand. We specialize in modern, elegant pieces—upholstered beds, occasional chairs, ottomans, benches and statement couches—all made to order in the colour and fabric of your choice.

Saw it on Pinterest? We can make it for your home. We work with you to bring your vision to life, with a focus on quality materials, clean lines and a warm, inviting aesthetic.

What we stand for

Custom design

Every piece is made from scratch to match your vision—size, fabric and finish.

Quality materials

We use quality upholstery and finishes for pieces that last and look beautiful.

Personal service

From first contact to delivery, we work with you to get the details right.
